AI Agent Auto-Evaluation
KPI Framework

A scoring guide for AI-powered transcript evaluation of PSI voice agents
Prepared: March 2026  |  Version 1.0

📡 Category 1 — Technical Performance
1
Response Delay / Latency
Multiplier ×1.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%Good (<1.5s)Agent responds immediately after client finishes; no lag indicators, no filler words used to stall.
80%Slight delayMinor phrases like "one moment" or "let me check" appear, but agent recovers and answers promptly.
50%Borderline (3–5s)Noticeable pause is implied; client had to repeat themselves or asked an additional prompt before a reply.
20%High (>5s)Client asked "are you there?", repeated themselves multiple times, or call showed signs of near-disconnect.
2
Noise & Background Chatter Handling
Multiplier ×1.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%Ignored properlyNo signs the agent reacted to background noise; stayed fully on topic throughout.
80%Minor hesitationBrief pause noted in transcript but agent quickly returned to subject.
50%Briefly disruptedAgent briefly addressed the wrong voice or went off-topic for one exchange.
20%Major disruptionAgent responded to someone other than the intended caller, lost thread of conversation.
📋 Category 2 — Agent Rules & Instructions
3
Prompt Adherence
Multiplier ×1.5
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%Fully adheredAll responses stay within persona and KB scope; no personal opinions or off-topic answers given.
80%Slightly deviatedOne minor step outside scope (e.g. gave an unsolicited suggestion) but quickly corrected course.
50%Partial driftMultiple off-script responses detected; agent started improvising answers not backed by KB.
20%Off-promptAgent operated clearly outside role (gave legal/financial opinions, changed persona, answered from personal knowledge).
4
Instructions Following
Multiplier ×1.5
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%All followedCorrect language used, honorifics (Mr./Ms.) applied, call flow respected, all do-not-say rules intact.
80%Mostly followedOne minor slip (e.g. skipped honorific once, minor flow deviation) but overall compliant.
50%Some missedSeveral rules broken (e.g. spoke wrong language, skipped a qualification step, ignored script flow).
20%ViolatedMajor rule broken — revealed it's an AI, spoke entirely in wrong language, disclosed prohibited info.
5
Intent / Entity Detection
Multiplier ×1.5
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%AccurateAll entities correctly captured and confirmed (name, budget, interest/unit type, location preference).
80%Mostly accurateOne entity slightly misheard or paraphrased but corrected later in the call.
50%PartialSeveral key entities left ambiguous or not followed up on despite being mentioned.
20%InaccurateWrong entities captured (e.g. wrong budget figure, wrong name recorded, wrong property type).
6
Sentiment / Frustration Reading
Multiplier ×1.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%CorrectAgent softened tone and applied objection handling when frustration signals appeared in the transcript.
80%Slightly misreadAcknowledged frustration but response was slightly off-tone or delayed one exchange.
50%OffMissed the emotional cue and continued pushing the same approach without adjustment.
20%MissedClient expressed clear frustration or desire to end call; agent completely ignored it and kept going.
7
Interest Level Detection
Multiplier ×1.5
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%CorrectCorrectly detected hot/warm/cold signals; closing approach and tag match what the transcript shows.
80%Mostly trackedInterest level mostly right, minor mismatch in closing tone or slightly premature close.
50%PartialSome interest signals missed; closing was premature or too late relative to signals given.
20%MissedInterest level tagged completely wrong compared to what the transcript conversation reveals.
8
Wrong Person Handling
Multiplier ×1.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%CorrectAgent politely confirmed identity, asked for the right person, or ended professionally per script.
80%Minor deviationDid the right thing but wording was slightly off the given wrong-person script.
50%PartialGot confused and tried to continue with the pitch to the wrong person for one exchange.
20%Wrong handlingDisclosed private call info to wrong person, pitched fully to wrong person, or ended call rudely.
📊 Category 3 — Data Accuracy
9
Outcome Tag Accuracy
Multiplier ×2.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%All correctTag applied perfectly matches call outcome (e.g. client said "book me in" → Interested/Meeting Booked).
80%Mostly correctClose match — e.g. tagged "Needs Follow-up" when "Interested" would be more accurate but understandable.
50%Partially correctTag is plausible but misses nuance or is incomplete (e.g. missed DNC signal in transcript).
20%Wrong / missingCompletely wrong tag applied OR no tag applied despite a clear outcome being reached in the call.
10
Overall Memory Fields Extraction
Multiplier ×2.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%All correctEvery required memory field is filled accurately (Sales: Name, Budget, Interest. Listing: Project, Type, Size, Price).
80%Mostly correctOne field slightly paraphrased or approximated but close enough to be usable.
50%PartialMultiple fields missing or inaccurate; key qualification data is not fully captured.
20%PoorMost fields are missing or contain completely wrong values compared to what was stated.
11
Hallucination Severity
Multiplier ×2.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%None detectedAgent only referenced verifiable KB content; no invented facts, names, or claims.
80%Very minorSlight rephrasing that changed meaning minimally (e.g. "about 900K" when KB says "starting from 850K").
50%Minor / Low riskInvented a soft claim without KB backing (e.g. "I believe the project is near the metro").
20%SignificantFabricated price, project name, amenity, payment plan, or made a false promise to close the call.
12
Price Invention
Multiplier ×2.0
ScoreLabelHow to Detect in Transcript
100%No invented pricesAgent only quoted exact KB prices or correctly declined to give a price and offered to connect with consultant.
80%Very minor roundingSlight rounding of an existing KB price but intent and direction were correct.
50%Conversion errorGot the AED/USD conversion wrong or mixed up payment plan figures.
20%Invented priceQuoted a price with zero KB basis; no corresponding figure exists in any project material.

🧮 Scoring Formula

Category Score  =  Σ (Question Score × Multiplier)  ÷  Σ (100% × Multiplier)

Technical Score  =  Average of KPIs 1–2
Rules Score      =  Average of KPIs 3–8
Data Score       =  Average of KPIs 9–12
Overall Score    =  Weighted average of all active categories

Grade Thresholds:
  ≥ 85%  →  ✓ Pass
  ≥ 70%  →  ~ Needs Review
  < 70%  →  ✕ Fail

Note: If a KPI is not triggered (no noise, price not raised, etc.), mark as N/A
      and exclude from both the earned score AND the maximum score.

📝 System Prompt Template for AI Evaluator Agent

"You are a QA evaluation agent. You will be given a call transcript between an AI voice agent (Sarah) and a client. Evaluate the agent's performance using the 12 KPIs below. For each KPI, select one of the four ratings (100%, 80%, 50%, or 20%) and provide a one-line justification quoting specific evidence from the transcript. If a KPI was not triggered during this call (e.g. no background noise, price was not raised), mark it as N/A and exclude it from scoring. At the end, calculate the Overall Score, the category sub-scores (Technical, Agent Rules, Data Accuracy), and the final Grade (Pass / Needs Review / Fail)."
